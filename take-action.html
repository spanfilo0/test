Find Your Voice

Purepecha is one out of the many indigenous languages throughout the world that has been silenced. People throughout the nation have become ashamed of their language and traditions. It is time to break that silence and recognize our roots. Being able to connect with your culture is what keeps your culture alive. People have to acknowledge other cultures and standing up against opression. Purepecha is part of my culture and by identifying with it, I give courage to the opressed to speak.

How Can I Find My Voice?

Once an item is completed, click the square to check it off!

  • Step 1: Ask your family about your roots.
  • Step 2: If you have access to the internet, research about your language, culture and its traditions.
  • Step 3: Learn about some of its struggles and problems.
  • Step 4: Find ways of how you can advocate for your culture and others in the same position.
